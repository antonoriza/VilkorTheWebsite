Frequently Asked Questions

Is Vilkor compatible with any type of property?

add
Yes. Vilkor is designed to adapt to residential, commercial, vertical (towers), and horizontal (gated communities) developments, providing specific tools for each operating model.

How long does implementation and migration take?

add
Our architecture allows for immediate startup. Base configuration is completed in hours, and the migration of historical data from other systems is typically finished in less than 48 business hours.

Is my data secure and compliant with regulations?

add
Absolutely. We use military-grade encryption (AES-256) and comply with SOC2 and GDPR standards, ensuring that asset and resident information is private and secure 24/7.

Do you offer specialized technical support?

add
Yes. Depending on your plan, you have access to support via email, live chat, or a dedicated Onboarding Architect who ensures the continuous success of your operation without intermediaries.

How do residents access the platform?

add
Vilkor is 100% web and mobile-responsive. Residents can manage payments, report incidents, and vote from any browser in seconds, without the need for complex installations.

What happens if I decide to leave the platform in the future?

add
Your data belongs entirely to you. Vilkor guarantees information sovereignty; you can export your financial and operational history in standard formats at any time, ensuring your administration is never held hostage by technology.

Are there forced contracts or minimum terms?

add
No. We offer total flexibility with monthly plans and no mandatory stay. For organizations looking to optimize their investment, the annual plan offers a 20% discount while maintaining the same operational freedom.
